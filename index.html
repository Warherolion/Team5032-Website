About Us

Team 5032 is Meadowvale Secondary School’s robotics team, located in Mississauga, Ontario, since our establishment in 2014, we have annually competed in the First Robotics Competition, even making it to the Ontario provincial competition in 2017.

As firm believers of providing all students with opportunities in the STEM and Business fields, we assist our local middle school’s First Lego League Team.

Our continued growth and success over the past 6 years is a reflection of the countless members, mentors, and sponsors that have supported throughout the years and allow us to positively impact our community by bringing people together through STEM.
